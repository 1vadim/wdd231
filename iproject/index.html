Neighbor Site Plan

This page outlines the planning and design decisions for

the Neighbor website project.

1. Site Name

Name: Neighbor

Why: The name "Neighbor" reflects a sense of support, connection, and community. It’s easy to remember and aligns well with the mission.

Suggested domain: neighbor-help.org

2. Site Purpose

The website will serve as a hub for information and connection, helping individuals and families in need find support and allowing community members to volunteer or donate easily. The site also offers a blog, event info, and contact tools.

3. Scenarios

  • How can I volunteer for upcoming events?
  • How can someone request assistance for food or housing?

4. Color Schema

Primary Color: Indigo (#5C6BC0)

Accent Color: Amber (#FDD835)

Usage:

  • Primary (Indigo) – headers, buttons, emphasis
  • Accent (Amber) – background highlights, section headers

5. Typography

  • Headings: Playfair Display
  • Body: Roboto
